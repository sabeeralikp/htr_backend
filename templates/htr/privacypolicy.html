Introduction

Welcome to "Dhriti OCR," a document text extraction and OCR tool developed by the International Centre for Free and Open-Source Software (hereinafter referred to as "ICFOSS," "we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the data obtained through the "Dhriti OCR" app.

Information We Collect

1. Document and Images: "Dhriti OCR" extracts text from documents and images provided by you. These documents, images and the extracted text copies are stored in our highly secured servers to improve accuracy and app performance.

2. User Feedback: We may collect user feedback to improve our services. User feedback may include comments, suggestions, and bug reports.

3. Third party Data Sharing: We ensure that the data you provide us with is not shared with other companies, organizations or any other third parties.

Data Usage

The data collected by "Dhriti OCR" is used for the following purposes:

1. Improving App Performance: Extracted text data is used to enhance the accuracy and functionality of the OCR tool, ensuring a better user experience.

2. Feedback and Support: User feedback is used to address issues, provide technical support, and make enhancements based on user suggestions.

Children's Privacy

"Dhriti OCR" is designed to be suitable for people of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13 without verifiable parental consent.
